Meet Our Organizers

These are the awesome organizations behind our charity activities

National Volunteer Network of Kazakhstan

The National Volunteer Network forms, supports and develops a culture of volunteerism, volunteer initiatives and volunteer activities among citizens, civil society institutions, the business sector and government agencies in Kazakhstan.

Best For Kids Public Foundation

A non-profit charitable organization that helps children and children in orphanages with social adaptation. You can support the project financially or by applying for a volunteer position on the foundation's website. By helping the project, you make a significant contribution to the development and future of children from orphanages.

The Mentors Project

The Mentors project of the Dara Charitable Foundation helps teenagers from orphanages to socialize through friendship with a mentor. The main task of the mentor is to become a friend who will share his experience and knowledge with the child from the orphanage for two years, as well as support and give advice. You can help the project by becoming a mentor to one of the children, having previously applied for participation in the project.

Public movement "Demeu"

Public movement to help the elderly, orphaned children, and single mothers with special children. Demeu volunteers also provide food assistance to mothers with many children without a head of family. You can support a social movement financially.

Istoki Dobra Charitable Foundation"

Non-profit charitable foundation for helping the elderly, people with disabilities, and low-income families. The Foundation provides assistance in the form of food, hot meals, medicines, and warm clothing. You can support a charitable foundation financially.

Public Foundation "Dar"

A charitable public foundation that aims to help orphaned children, children with disabilities, and children in need of treatment. You can support the foundation financially, as well as with services, items, advice, and suggestions.
